About

I am a mechanical engineer with a foundation in aerospace engineering and advanced training in mechanical engineering and engineering science. My work sits at the intersection of design of mechanical systems, non-destructive techniques (ultrasonics testing), and machine learning — developing and improving systems to understand materials at the process level.

At Penn State I built an in-situ measurement system for ceramic cold-sintering. Before that, at University Transportation Center for Railway Safety (UTCRS) at University of Texas Rio Grande Valley (UTRGV), I applied signal processing and ML to railway bearing diagnostics. I am passionate about designing and building systems while turning complex sensor data from these systems into actionable engineering insight.
